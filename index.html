Challenges in research

During our research we heard that recruiting users was a challenge, especially for small teams with limited budget. Recruiting users from a diverse background was particularly challenging.

We observed that getting consent forms setup was time consuming and sometimes confusing.

We also heard that people often want someone to help them design their research. Or they might want someone to do a sense check on their research questions. However, they don't always have people inside their organisation that can help.

This prototype is a way for us to learn more about these challenges. Its also a way for us to test if we can help in any way. As this is a prototype, what you see here will change as we test and learn.
